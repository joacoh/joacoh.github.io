Academic talk presented at multiple venues (ESO Chile, PUC, UDP and UdeC) during my visit to Chile in March 2026 talking about my first project of my PhD. In this talk I presented the results of the homonimus paper describeing the ionised and molecular phases of the outflow in the starburst galaxy ESO 484-036, all of this as part of the GECKOS collaboration. This talk endedup being my Confirmation of Candidature (CoC) talk for my PhD. The slides shared here correspond to the CoC version of the talk. All confidential information was removed from the slides.
